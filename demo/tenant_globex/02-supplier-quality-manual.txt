# === GLOBEX SUPPLIER QUALITY MANUAL (synthetic, IATF-style) ===

## 1. Scope

This manual describes the Quality Management System used by Globex
Manufacturing for production parts supplied to automotive OEMs.

## 2. Document control

Controlled documents are stored in the QMS portal. Superseded revisions are
retained for ten years.

## 3. Control of nonconforming product

Nonconforming product is segregated in the NCR cage and reviewed by the
Material Review Board within one business day. Decisions: rework, repair,
scrap, or concession.

## 4. Corrective actions

Every NCR triggers an 8D corrective action. The default closure target is
**30 calendar days**. Customer-impact NCRs are reported within 24 hours via
the customer portal.

## 5. Audit

Internal audits cover every process at least annually. External audits by
customers and certification bodies are welcomed.
