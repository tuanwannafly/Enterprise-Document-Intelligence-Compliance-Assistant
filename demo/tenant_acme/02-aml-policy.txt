# === ACME ANTI-MONEY-LAUNDERING POLICY (synthetic) ===

Effective date: 2026-01-15. Owner: Compliance Officer, ACME.

## 1. Customer Identification Program (CIP)

All new customers must provide the following at onboarding:

* Full legal name
* Date of birth
* Residential address
* Government identifier (passport, driver's licence, or SSN)
* Source-of-funds declaration

## 2. Suspicious Activity Reporting (SAR)

Any transaction exceeding **USD 10,000** in a single business day, or any
pattern of transactions that raises concern, must be escalated to the
Compliance Officer within 24 hours. SARs are confidential; do not disclose
the existence of a SAR to the customer.

## 3. Sanctions screening

Every counterparty is screened against the latest OFAC sanctions list at
onboarding and quarterly thereafter. Contact Compliance at
sanctions@acme-fsi.example for the screening workflow.

## 4. Training

All staff complete annual AML training. Records are retained for five years.
